FAQs

  • What is meant by "variants" of COVID-19?
    •  A variant is a viral genome(gentic code) that contains one or more mutations that diffrentiates it from the original virus.
    Due to these mutations these variants also have different properties and they affect the human body differently as compared to the original virus and other variants.
  • Is COVID-19 an airborne disease?
    •  Yes,COVID-19 can spread via airborne transmission which means that people diagnosed with COVID-19 generate tiny liquid particles
    that contain the live virus when they exhale,talk or cough for example .When people take in these particles through their nose or mouth there is a high chance they can get infected with COVID-19
  • How is Omicron different from Delta variant of COVID-19?
    •  Omicron is more transmissible than the delta variant and is bound to affect a larget amount of the population
    but causes less serious diseases than the delta variant according to studies done by WHO.
